Diagnose on your own!...

There is a lack of information by many farmers in regard to how pests and diseases can be diagnosed and fought by commercial sugar cane farmers in Uganda (Diemer et al., 2020; Seguya et al., 2017). This system offers a quick to understand and diagnose sugarcane diseases. After diagnosis, the farmer is advised on the management of the identified disease.
